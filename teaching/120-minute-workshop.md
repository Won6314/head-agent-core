# 120-Minute Workshop

[HEAD Agent Core](../README.md) / [Teach](README.md) / 120-Minute Workshop

## Outcome

Participants apply the full architecture to a synthetic, durable outcome. They produce a bounded work model with decision rights, context sources, evidence gates, a durable agreement, and a minimal adoption recommendation.

## Preparation

- Form groups of three to five with mixed disciplines where possible.
- Use the fictional [Library Kiosk Pilot](../learn/08-operation/end-to-end-example.md), or an equally fictional scenario with no private facts.
- Open [canonical diagrams](diagrams.md) and the [workshop questions](discussion-questions.md#workshop-exercises).

## Run Of Show

| Time | Activity | Deliverable | Coverage |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 0-10 min | Frame the thesis and evidence labels | Shared definition of controlled expansion and the difference between Historical record, Operational observation, Generalized failure, and Related theory. | [Course Thesis](../learn/README.md#course-thesis), [Evidence Labels](../learn/README.md#evidence-labels) |
| 10-25 min | Map the problem | Identify where unchecked expansion could compound a guess; place an evidence gate. | [One-Step Expansion](../learn/02-llm-problem/the-one-step-expansion-rule.md), [Verification](../learn/08-operation/verification.md), [Controlled expansion loop](diagrams.md#controlled-expansion-loop) |
| 25-40 min | Assign decision rights | Separate User material choices, HEAD planning and integration, and Agent local methods. | [Decision Rights](../learn/03-ownership/decision-rights.md), [Decision-rights escalation](diagrams.md#decision-rights-escalation) |
| 40-55 min | Compose context | List project-owned authority, what HEAD needs to judge the whole outcome, and the smallest complete worker brief. | [Context By Ownership](../learn/04-context/context-by-ownership.md), [Context diagram](diagrams.md#context-by-ownership) |
| 55-65 min | Break | No deliverable. | |
| 65-80 min | Preserve the agreement | Separate session identity, the full run agreement, and subordinate retrieval records; draft the run's problem, goal, scope, success conditions, decisions, assumptions, current position, next action, and evidence locations. | [Context And Run](../learn/06-canon/context-and-run.md), [Fragile Progress And History](../learn/06-canon/fragile-progress-and-history.md), [Durable work agreement](diagrams.md#durable-work-agreement) |
| 80-95 min | Compose the system | Decide which of Core, project context, runtime canon, Skills, MCP, and Agents are needed now. | [How The Parts Compose](../learn/07-components/how-the-parts-compose.md) |
| 95-105 min | Challenge alternatives | Compare a swarm, a context dump, deny-list growth, or summary-as-canon with the group model; name its limitation. | [Generative Rules](../learn/05-general-rules/generative-rules.md), [Decisions](../learn/09-decisions/README.md), [Simplification After Complexity](../learn/10-evolution/simplification-after-complexity.md) |

Total: 120 minutes.

## Group Worksheet

Each group writes only the following, in plain language:

1. The user-fixed goal and one material decision that must not be inferred.
2. One coherent next outcome, its owner, and direct completion evidence.
3. The smallest authoritative context needed by that owner.
4. The evidence gate before this result becomes input to later work.
5. The session identity and full-run facts needed to resume the whole outcome, with progress and history kept subordinate.
6. The smallest adoption level that fits the scenario, plus one limitation.

## Facilitator Checks

- Reject a step list that has no independently observable outcome; reshape it as a bounded result.
- Return material direction to the User rather than letting HEAD or an Agent infer it.
- Ask for direct evidence rather than a status claim.
- Keep progress and history as retrieval records; do not let them replace session identity or the full run agreement.
- Keep project-owned facts out of shared Core guidance.
- Treat theoretical language as interpretation unless the course labels it as Historical record.

## Debrief

Compare group outputs with the [synthetic end-to-end example](../learn/08-operation/end-to-end-example.md). The goal is not to reproduce its scenario or add every component. The goal is to apply the same authority, canon, component-boundary, and verification logic at the appropriate scale.
