## Why It Exists

HEAD needs to know what authoritative sources exist before it can retrieve the right one. The index keeps source locations, repository boundaries, capability entry points, and canonical-document routes discoverable without embedding their contents.

## Boundary

The index points to knowledge; it is not a substitute for the knowledge. Material decisions should be grounded in the referenced source rather than an index description.
