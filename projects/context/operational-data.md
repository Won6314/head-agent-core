## Why It Exists

Live behavior and user outcomes cannot always be inferred from code or plans. Operational databases and analytics provide current evidence while remaining outside the always-loaded context.

## Boundary

The project owns schemas, credentials, privacy rules, query interfaces, and mutation policy. Shared HEAD behavior does not grant access or authorize writes.
