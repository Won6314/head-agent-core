## Why It Exists

Design files, planning systems, communication tools, and other workspaces may hold evidence required for an outcome. They are indexed as project sources rather than copied into the shared Core.

## Boundary

Access, publication, messaging, and external mutation remain governed by project policy and explicit user authority.
