## Why It Exists

Product behavior, policy, user flows, and domain relationships are project facts. Keeping them in canonical project sources allows HEAD to retrieve only the product slice relevant to the current outcome.

## Boundary

The shared layer defines how evidence is composed, not what the product means. Product authority and updates remain with the project.
