## Why It Exists

Deployment, service ownership, credentials, incident procedures, and environment details are operational project knowledge. Keeping them in project-controlled sources limits exposure and lets the information evolve independently of the shared HEAD package.

## Boundary

The shared layer does not authorize deployment or production mutation. Projects define their own operational controls and approval requirements.
